About VeraGen Therapeutics

Founded in 2003, VeraGen Therapeutics is a global biopharmaceutical company dedicated to discovering, developing, and delivering innovative medicines that address serious health challenges.

Our team of over 5,000 scientists, researchers, and healthcare professionals work across 15 research facilities worldwide, united by a shared mission to improve patient outcomes.

We invest significantly in research and development, with over $800 million invested annually in discovering the next generation of treatments.
