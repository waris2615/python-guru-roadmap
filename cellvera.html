About Cellvera Pharmaceuticals

Founded in 1998, Cellvera Pharmaceuticals has grown from a small research company into a global pharmaceutical leader. Our mission is simple: to develop medicines that meaningfully improve patients' lives.

We combine cutting-edge science with a deep understanding of patient needs to create treatments that make a real difference. Our portfolio spans therapeutic areas including oral health, cardiovascular disease, neuroscience, and immunology.

Headquartered in the United States, we operate research facilities and distribution centers worldwide, employing over 3,000 dedicated professionals.
